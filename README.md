## 📸 Overview

<img width="1866" height="875" alt="image" src="https://github.com/user-attachments/assets/b755b0bb-9265-49f7-bbdf-3445570a6969" />


CVPilot turns a generic CV into a role-specific, ATS-optimised application in one click. Paste a job description, and CVPilot scores your CV against it, highlights gaps, and tailors your content to match — so more of your applications actually reach a human.

## ✨ Features

- **🎯 ATS Score** — instant analysis of how well a CV matches a given job description, with a clear score and the keywords you're missing.
- **⚡ One-Click Tailoring** — automatically rewrites and reorders your CV to fit a specific role while keeping it truthful to your experience.
- **🗄️ Master CV Vault** — store one master CV and spin off unlimited tailored versions without losing the original.
- **📋 Job Tracker** — a built-in Kanban board to track applications from saved → applied → interview → offer.
- **🎨 Templates** — a library of professional, ATS-safe templates.
- **📤 Multi-Format Export** — export to PDF, DOCX, and more.

## 🧠 How it works

1. **Upload** your master CV (or build one in the editor).
2. **Paste** the job description for a role you want.
3. CVPilot **analyses** the match, scores it, and surfaces missing keywords and gaps.
4. **One click** tailors your CV to the role; review, tweak, and export.
5. **Track** the application in the job board.
